About Precision Agriculture AI Dashboard (PAS)

PAS is a research-oriented Precision Agriculture System that combines AI models, satellite/drone imagery and feedback-oriented dashboards to support data-driven farm decisions.

Precision Agriculture Concept

Site-specific management of water, nutrients and crop protection using field-level data instead of one-size-fits-all recommendations.

AI & Machine Learning

Crop recommendation, disease classification and yield analytics are powered by data-driven models aligned with Indian cropping systems.

Feedback-Oriented Dashboards

Each interaction feeds back into PAS, enabling continuous refinement of recommendations and visual analytics for farmers and researchers.

Core Modules
  • AI-powered crop recommendation aligned with soil and climate conditions
  • Crop disease detection for both mobile and drone images, with treatment guidance
  • Yield analytics and profit tracking through interactive charts and production logs
  • Drone and satellite-based field analysis with stress visualization
  • Weather-aware chatbot for PIN-based irrigation and protection advice
Research Alignment

This dashboard is built as part of the research work "Precision Agriculture Through AI and Feedback-Oriented Dashboards". It is designed as a prototype decision support system and not a final commercial product.

The architecture is intentionally modular so additional models (NDVI analytics, yield prediction, input price feeds, scheme alerts) can be plugged in without changing the overall PAS design.
